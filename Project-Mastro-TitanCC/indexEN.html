INNOVATION
Differentials applied in projects

Vacuum Infusion

The catamarans are produced by vacuum infusion and both hull and deck are sandwiched in PVC, in a wood-free construction. All this care gives the shipyard the security of building very high quality boats, which will maintain their attributes for many years to come. Laminates manufactured by the process are more resistant than conventional laminates manufactured by hand, as their impregnation between the fibers reaches 60%, while in manual lamination it only reaches 20 to 30% of the fiber content. With the infusion method, we leave aside environmental and unhealthy problems, as there is no wastage of material and there is no direct contact between the builder and the resins

Background Fishing

As the name implies, this type of fishing is carried out in deep places. It consists of placing the bait, preferably natural ones, at the bottom. The boat used must have space for movement and work with the fish.

Offshore or Ocean Fishing

For this modality, boats with powerful engines are recommended to cover long distances in long-term fishing.

Sea Fishing

In this modality, the fisherman uses special weapons to shoot down fish underwater. Large distances are usually covered and a lot of space is needed to store all the equipment.
